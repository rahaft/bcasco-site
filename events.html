Events

Upcoming activities and gatherings for our members and friends.

Monthly Social Lunch

First Tuesday of every month · 12:00 PM · Community Hall

Join us for a relaxed lunch with friends, conversation, and light entertainment. Please call ahead if you need transportation.

Gentle Exercise Class

Every Thursday · 10:00 AM · Activity Room

A chair-based exercise class designed for all ability levels. Wear comfortable clothes and supportive shoes.

Game Afternoon

Third Friday of every month · 1:00 PM · Lounge

Cards, board games, and puzzles. Bring a friend or meet someone new. Light refreshments provided.
